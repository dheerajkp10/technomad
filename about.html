About Me

Dheeraj Kumar Paras

Builder, mentor and engineering leader with a passion for scalable systems and human stories.

Summary

I’m a Software Development Manager at Amazon Web Services with over a decade of experience building scalable, highly available and performant software solutions. I currently lead a team of more than 8 engineers on the AWS Lambda data plane, managing critical infrastructure that handles billions of serverless function invocations daily with 99.99% availability and sub‑millisecond latency.

Throughout my career I’ve designed large‑scale distributed systems, traffic routing architectures and resilient data plane services that scale from zero to millions of concurrent executions. I’ve previously contributed to personalization and recommendation systems at Amazon, driving millions of customer engagements and generating millions in annual revenue.

Beyond technical delivery, I’m passionate about building strong teams. I’ve hired over 55 engineers and mentored 12+, nurturing ownership and excellence across organizations. I’m an immigrant who has navigated cultural change from India to the U.S., a nomad who has explored 15+ countries, and a storyteller who believes that our personal journeys shape our professional growth.
