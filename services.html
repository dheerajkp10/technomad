Services

Empower Your Journey

Whether you’re preparing for your next interview, architecting complex systems or planning your long‑term career, I offer tailored services to help you succeed.

What I Offer

Mock Coding Interviews

Practice FAANG‑style coding interviews with real‑time feedback on your approach, coding style and problem solving techniques. Sessions are designed to simulate the pressure and format of actual interviews so you can perform confidently when it matters.

System Design Interviews

Learn to design robust, scalable systems through open‑ended problems tailored to your experience level. We’ll cover distributed architectures, data modelling, fault tolerance and trade‑off analysis to help you articulate solutions clearly and convincingly.

Resume & Outreach

Get a detailed resume review from a hiring manager’s perspective. I’ll help you highlight impact, quantify achievements and craft targeted outreach messages to catch the eye of recruiters and hiring managers.

Behavioral Coaching

Master the STAR (Situation–Task–Action–Result) method and tell compelling stories that showcase your leadership, collaboration and problem‑solving skills. We’ll refine your storytelling so you connect authentically with interviewers.

Career Planning

Develop a personalised roadmap for growth, whether you’re aiming for technical mastery, people management or entrepreneurship. We’ll assess your strengths and values, set clear milestones and identify skills to cultivate.

1:1 Mentorship

Receive ongoing mentorship sessions tailored to your goals. From navigating internal politics to sharpening your technical vision, I’ll provide candid guidance and accountability to accelerate your journey.
